"The Natural State"

Arkansas' nickname is The Natural State. It has many unknown spots that bolster its natural beauty, many of which can make for a great day trip destination.

A culturally rich University

Fayetteville, nestled in the Northwest corner of the state, is home to the University of Arkansas. With nearly 40,000 students year-round, the area has become a thriving community complete with cultural diversity, growing businesses, and home to the wildly popular sports teams of the University, the Arkansas Razorbacks.
